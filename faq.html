FAQ

Answers to common questions from homeowners

We keep our answers clear and practical so you can quickly understand how to move forward.

What pests do you typically help with?

PowerGuard Pest focuses on common residential pest concerns such as ants, spiders, cockroaches, termites, mosquitoes, rodents, and other seasonal household pests.

How quickly can I request service?

You can call, email, or submit a form request at any time. Response timing depends on service demand and availability, but the site is built to make that first step fast and simple.

Do you offer emergency pest services?

Yes. Emergency pest response is available for urgent situations. Availability may vary depending on your location and current scheduling demand.

How much does a pest inspection cost?

Our pricing section includes a starting inspection price of $99. Final pricing can vary depending on the property, pest type, and service scope.

Will I receive a clear estimate before service?

Yes. We aim to provide transparent service guidance and pricing information before work is scheduled, with recommendations based on what you share and what the inspection reveals.

Do you guarantee complete pest elimination?

No service provider can ethically guarantee identical results in every property or environment. We avoid misleading claims and focus on practical treatment plans, monitoring, and follow-up guidance.

Can I contact you by phone instead of using the form?

Absolutely. Tap the click-to-call links throughout the site to call (844) 491-0203 directly.

Where are you located?
