About Us

Professional, transparent, and homeowner-focused pest service

PowerGuard Pest is built around one simple goal: helping U.S. homeowners quickly connect with pest control support they can trust.

Our Story

Helping protect homes with clear communication and dependable support

PowerGuard Pest was created for homeowners who want a straightforward, trustworthy way to request help for common household pest issues. Instead of overcomplicating the process, we focus on fast contact, honest recommendations, and practical service information.

Our website experience is intentionally simple: clear navigation, transparent pricing guidance, easy click-to-call options, and contact forms that make it easier for customers to take the next step.

Why Homeowners Reach Out

Common reasons customers contact PowerGuard Pest

Recurring indoor pests

Ants, spiders, roaches, silverfish, and occasional invaders can return season after season without a targeted treatment plan.

Visible rodent activity

Hearing movement in walls, finding droppings, or seeing entry gaps often prompts homeowners to seek quick inspection support.

Termite concerns

Early signs of termite activity can be easy to miss, which is why inspections and prevention guidance remain a major focus.

Outdoor pest pressure

Mosquitoes, wasps, and yard-related pest activity can affect comfort, safety, and routine use of outdoor spaces.
